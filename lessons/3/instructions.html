Since this model can't predict these data perfectly, you'd better find a new game. Here are two games to try:

Log-likelihood game. Try to predict the data "as well as you can." How big can you make the log-likelihood of your model (shown by the horizontal gray bar)? This measures how well your model predicts the training data.

Matching game. Even if you can't match all 4 probabilities with your sliders, maybe you can match the 4 features. That is, try to make your model predict that among 60 shape tokens, you would have

  • 40 circle shapes     (both solid and striped)
  • 20 triangle shapes  (both solid and striped)
  • 40 solid shapes       (both circles and triangles)
  • 20 striped shapes    (both circles and triangles)
just as in the training data.
